Qualification Outcomes

What Your Result Means

Our DTI-based engine classifies your application into one of three outcomes.

DTI < 35%

Fully Qualified

Your debt-to-income ratio is ideal. Lenders are highly likely to approve.

  • Strong income-to-debt position
  • Meets standard lender thresholds
  • Proceed to formal application
Most Common
DTI 35–42%

Conditionally Qualified

Your ratio is elevated but manageable with compensating factors.

  • Borderline DTI range
  • Debt reduction recommended
  • Agent review suggested
DTI > 42%

Not Qualified

Your debt load exceeds safe lending limits. Improvement plan needed.

  • DTI exceeds lender limits
  • Income increase may help
  • Reassess in 6–12 months
